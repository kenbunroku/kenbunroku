Flectere

“Flexible” is derived from Flectere in Latin.
Flectere is a personal working desk which can switch between a standing mode and a sitting mode fast and flexibly, by detaching the upper part.
This is also so economic that users can put their stuff on the middle board or in the base, and comfortably put elbows on the top when they work.

About

Making an office furniture design with foam core boards in a design foundation class of a graduate school.

Problem

How might I show a solution for an argument that which working style is better, standing or sitting.

Time

4 weeks

Role

Designer of a personal project
