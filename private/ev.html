EV Spec. Requirement Analysis

Introduction

This project was to understand customers' expectation for Electric Vehicle (EV) specification such as its price, driving range and charging time. The R&D Dept. of Toyota needed the information because the company had not introduced authentic EV before. To introduce the 1st global model, they were required to understand which specification would be prioritized by costomers and how much.

For solving the problem, I conducted conjoint research that was one of the quantitative research in 9 countries, and built a simulator that the engineers set valuables and saw how many costemers could accept the spec. As a result, the R&D Dept. simulated more than 1,000 variations that were taken into consideration for their plan.
